## About

**SAYANJALI BLOCKCHAIN** is the open-source infrastructure layer being built to connect and settle usage across the **SAYANJALI NEXUS** product ecosystem — a growing suite of AI tools, SaaS platforms, and automation products.

This is not a meme token and not a speculative side project. It's a settlement and identity layer designed around use cases that already exist across SAYANJALI NEXUS: AI subscriptions, automation workflows, developer services, and business tooling. Every core component is developed in the open.

> This project is under active, early-stage development. See [Disclaimer](#disclaimer) before relying on anything in this repository or its published materials.

## Why SAYANJALI BLOCKCHAIN

As an AI and automation product company grows, each new product tends to accumulate its own billing system, its own notion of identity, and its own usage ledger — fragmenting the experience across products and making usage hard to audit from the outside.

SAYANJALI BLOCKCHAIN addresses this with a single, open settlement and identity layer that every SAYANJALI NEXUS product can plug into, and that anyone can inspect:

- **Global adoption** — infrastructure designed for developers and businesses everywhere
- **Decentralized infrastructure** — no single point of control or failure
- **Real-world business utility** — built to settle actual usage, not speculation
- **Open-source innovation** — every core component published and auditable

## The SYJ Utility Token

SYJ is the unit of account for the SAYANJALI NEXUS ecosystem — designed to be used, not merely held. Specific tokenomics (total supply, allocation, and distribution schedule) will be published ahead of any public sale; they are intentionally not fixed at this stage.

Intended utility of SYJ includes:

- AI subscriptions across SAYANJALI NEXUS products
- SaaS licensing for platform access
- Marketplace payments between ecosystem participants
- Governance participation in network decisions
- Premium API access for developers
- Education platform access
- Developer services and tooling
- Enterprise automation billing
- Staking *(planned)*
- Validator rewards *(planned)*
